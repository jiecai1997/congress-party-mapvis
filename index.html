Congress Party US Choropleth

Distibution of Political Parties in US Congress overtime.

Question

The ideologies of Democratic and Republican parties in the US have changed drastically overtime, also leading to a huge difference in congress party representation throughout US history. We wanted to create a visualization that can show this trend.

Congress Dataset

We were given congress datasets that contained information about individual votes, as well as information about all the serving legistators and senators. We focused specifically on the parties of people who served in congress.

Our Choropleth

Our team proposed that a simple choropleth would be the most efficient in answering our question. Choropleth specifically can show how the congress's political party makeup has changed across different states. Our visualization was made using Datamaps and d3.js.
